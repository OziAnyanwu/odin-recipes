Lemon Dill Salmon

Description

A light and healthy oven-baked salmon fillet infused with fresh citrus and aromatic herbs.

Ingredients

  • 2 salmon fillets
  • 1 fresh lemon, sliced
  • 2 tablespoons fresh dill, chopped
  • 1 tablespoon melted butter

Steps

  1. Preheat your oven to 400°F (200°C).
  2. Place salmon on a parchment-lined tray and brush with melted butter.
  3. Top with lemon slices and dill, then bake for 12-15 minutes until flaky.
