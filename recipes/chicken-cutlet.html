Crispy Chicken Cutlets

Description

Thinly pounded chicken breasts coated in seasoned breadcrumbs and fried until perfectly crisp and tender.

Ingredients

  • 2 large chicken breasts, halved and pounded thin
  • 1 cup Panko breadcrumbs
  • 2 eggs, beaten
  • 1/2 cup all-purpose flour

Steps

  1. Dredge each cutlet in flour, dip in egg wash, and coat thoroughly with breadcrumbs.
  2. Heat oil in a large skillet over medium-high heat.
  3. Fry the cutlets for 3-4 minutes per side until the coating is golden and the chicken is cooked through.
