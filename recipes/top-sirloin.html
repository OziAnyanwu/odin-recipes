Top Sirloin

Description

Delicious high protein top sirloin steak. Eat daily for massive gains.

Ingredients

  • 4oz top sirloin steak
  • Salt and Pepper
  • Olive oil
  • 2 Tbsp of unsalted butter
  • 6 garlic cloves

Steps

  1. Season top sirloin generously with salt and peper
  2. Heat fying pan on medium-high heat
  3. Pour olive oil on frying pan, wait till it moves freely and you see a shimmer.
  4. Place one side of the season top-sirloin in the pan. Let sear for 1 minute
  5. Flip top sirloin over to sear the other side for 1 minute
  6. Take sirloin out momentarily, add butter and garlic cloves to the pan
  7. Put steak back in the pan for 3-4mins, basting it periodically
  8. Add store bought or home-made chimchurry sauce.
  9. Enjoy!
