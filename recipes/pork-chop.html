Honey Garlic Pork Chops

Description

Succulent, bone-in pork chops seared to a golden brown and finished with a sweet and savory garlic glaze.

Ingredients

  • 4 bone-in pork chops
  • 3 tablespoons honey
  • 2 cloves garlic, minced
  • 1 tablespoon olive oil

Steps

  1. Season the pork chops generously with salt and pepper.
  2. Sear in a hot skillet with olive oil for 5 minutes per side.
  3. Add honey and garlic to the pan, basting the chops until the sauce thickens.
